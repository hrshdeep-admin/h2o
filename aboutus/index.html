Our Story

Why I Started H2O

H2O Pressure Washing started with one simple goal: give every homeowner and business in the Okanagan access to professional exterior cleaning without paying a heavy price tag. Too many people were stuck choosing between a dirty property and an overpriced quote — I wanted to change that.

As a one-person company, I personally handle every job from quote to final rinse. No subcontractors, no call centers, no corner-cutting — just careful work and direct accountability to every customer I serve.

My goal is simple — to make our city shining and beautiful, one property at a time. Yashtaj, Founder of H2O Pressure Washing
